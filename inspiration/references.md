# Design Inspiration References

Curated list of websites and design patterns for inspiration. Populate before building design directions.

## How to Use
Research agency/studio websites that match the client's industry and desired aesthetic. For each reference, note:
- URL
- What's effective (layout, typography, color, interaction)
- What to borrow or adapt for this project
